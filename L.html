📜 Terms of Service

Last updated: March 2026

By accessing or using DevMarket you agree to these Terms. Please read carefully.

1. Account Registration

Provide accurate information at registration. You are responsible for all activity under your account. One account per person.

2. Marketplace Rules

Sellers retain ownership of their work but grant DevMarket a licence to distribute purchased items to buyers. All code must be original or properly licensed. Prohibited: malware, stolen code, IP violations.

3. Payments & Fees

DevMarket charges a 5% commission on all outgoing wallet transfers and payouts. Purchases use your wallet balance. Wallet top-ups are initiated by the buyer through the payment provider.

4. Prohibited Conduct

Users may not circumvent platform fees, harass others, upload malicious code, manipulate ratings, or use the platform for illegal activity.

5. Termination

We reserve the right to suspend accounts that violate these terms. Purchased items remain accessible after termination.

🔒 Privacy Policy

How we handle your data

We collect the minimum data needed to operate the platform: account information (email, username), transaction records, and usage analytics. We do not sell your data to third parties.

Data We Collect

  • Account: email, username, display name, bio, avatar
  • Transactions: purchase history, payout records, wallet balance
  • Usage: product views, search queries (anonymised)

Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your data at any time by contacting support. Accounts can be deleted from your profile settings.

⚖️ Rules & Guidelines

Community standards and best practices

For Sellers

  • Original Work: Only sell code you created or have rights to sell.
  • Quality: Products must be functional, documented, and free of critical bugs.
  • Accurate Listings: State requirements, dependencies, and limitations honestly.
  • Respond: Reply to buyer messages within a reasonable time.

For Buyers

  • License Compliance: Respect the licence attached to each purchase.
  • No Redistribution: Purchased items may not be resold or shared publicly.
  • Fair Reviews: Leave honest, constructive feedback after purchase.

Enforcement

Violations may result in content removal, account suspension, or permanent ban and forfeiture of earnings.

❓ Help Center

Frequently asked questions

Getting Started

How do I create an account?
Click "Get Started" in the header, fill in your username, email and password. No credit card required to browse.

Can I buy and sell?
Yes. Every account has full access to both purchasing and selling.

Purchasing

How do I buy something?
Fund your wallet first, then add items to cart and checkout. The amount is deducted from your wallet balance instantly.

Where are my purchases?
In your Library. Download links are always available there.

Selling

How do I list a product?
Go to "List Product" in the sidebar, upload your files and cover image via Cloudinary, fill in the details, and publish.

When do I get paid?
Sales are credited to your wallet instantly (minus the 5% platform commission). You can withdraw to your bank account at any time.

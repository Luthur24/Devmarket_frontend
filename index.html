Terms of Service — March 2026

By using DevMarket you agree to these terms.

1. Account

Provide accurate information. One account per person. You are responsible for all activity under your account.

2. Marketplace Rules

Only sell code you own or have rights to sell. Prohibited: malware, stolen code, IP violations.

3. Payments & Fees

DevMarket charges a 5% commission on outgoing wallet transfers and payouts. Purchases use your wallet balance.

4. Conduct

No harassment, fraudulent reviews, fee circumvention, or illegal use of the platform.

5. Termination

We may suspend accounts that violate these terms. Purchased items remain accessible.

Privacy Policy

We collect minimum data to operate: account info (email, username, avatar), transaction records, and anonymised usage analytics. We do not sell your data.

Your Rights

Request access, correction, or deletion of your data any time via profile settings or by contacting support.

Rules & Guidelines

For Sellers

  • Original Work: Only sell code you created or have rights to sell.
  • Quality: Products must be functional and documented.
  • Honesty: State dependencies and limitations clearly.
  • Respond: Reply to buyer messages in reasonable time.

For Buyers

  • License: Respect the licence on each purchase.
  • No Redistribution: Do not resell or share purchased items publicly.
  • Fair Reviews: Leave honest, constructive feedback.

Violations

Content removal, suspension, or permanent ban and forfeiture of earnings.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Tap "Get Started" on the Info → Home tab or in the top bar. Fill in username, email and password.

How do I buy something?

Fund your wallet (Wallet tab → Add Funds), browse products, add to cart, and checkout.

Where are my purchases?

In the Library section. Download links are always there.

How do I list a product?

Go to List Product in the menu. Upload your file and cover image, fill in details, publish.

When do I get paid?

Sales are credited to your wallet instantly (minus the 5% commission). Withdraw to your bank any time from the Wallet section.

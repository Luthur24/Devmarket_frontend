ℹ️ About Devmarket

Our mission and story

Devmarket was founded in 2024 by a team of developers who were frustrated with the fragmented landscape of code marketplaces. We believed that developers deserved a platform built with their needs in mind — a place where code quality matters, where transactions are secure, and where the community thrives.

Our platform serves as a bridge between developers who create valuable tools, templates, and resources, and those who need them to accelerate their projects. Whether you're a solo developer looking to monetize your side project or a team searching for the perfect starter template, Devmarket provides the infrastructure to make it happen.

2. Account Registration

You must provide accurate, current, and complete information during registration. You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your account credentials and for all activities that occur under your account.

3. Marketplace Rules

Sellers retain ownership of their creations but grant Devmarket a license to distribute purchased items to buyers. All code must be original or properly licensed. We reserve the right to suspend or terminate accounts that violate these terms. Upon termination, you lose access to the platform but retain rights to content you've purchased.

🔒 Privacy Policy

How we handle your data

At Devmarket, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

Information We Collect

Account Information: Email address, username, profile information, and payment details (processed securely through Stripe/PayPal).

Usage Data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, time spent on platform, and interaction patterns.

Transaction Data: Purchase history, sales records, and payout information.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our services
  • To process transactions and send payouts
  • To communicate updates, security alerts, and promotional offers (optional)
  • To detect and prevent fraud and abuse
  • To improve our platform through analytics

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption at rest and in transit, regular security audits, and strict access controls. Your payment information is never stored on our servers.

Your Rights

You have the right to: access your data, request corrections, delete your account, export your data, and opt-out of marketing communications. Contact privacy@devmarket.io for data-related requests.

⚖️ Rules & Guidelines

Community standards and best practices

Devmarket thrives because of our community's commitment to quality and integrity. These guidelines ensure a positive experience for everyone.

For Sellers

  • Original Work: Only upload code you have created or have explicit rights to sell
  • Quality Standards: All items must be functional, documented, and free of critical bugs
  • Accurate Descriptions: Clearly state requirements, dependencies, and limitations
  • Support Commitment: Respond to buyer inquiries within 48 hours
  • No Plagiarism: Duplicating others' work will result in permanent ban

For Buyers

  • License Compliance: Respect the license terms attached to each purchase
  • No Redistribution: Purchased items cannot be resold or shared publicly
  • Fair Reviews: Provide honest, constructive feedback
  • Security Responsibility: Review code before deployment in production

Prohibited Content

The following are strictly prohibited: malware or malicious code, stolen or pirated software, content violating third-party IP rights, automated purchasing bots, and any material promoting illegal activities.

Enforcement

Violations may result in: content removal, account suspension, permanent bans, and forfeiture of earnings. We reserve the right to remove any content at our discretion.

❓ Help Center

Frequently asked questions and support

Getting Started

How do I create an account?
Click "Get Started" in the header, fill in your email and password, and verify your email address. No credit card required to browse.

Can I be both a buyer and seller?
Absolutely. Every account has full access to both purchasing and selling features. Switch between modes from your dashboard.

Purchasing

What payment methods are accepted?
We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and cryptocurrency (BTC, ETH). All transactions are secured with 256-bit SSL encryption.

How do I access my purchases?
After payment confirmation, items appear in your Library with permanent download access. We also email you a secure download link.

Selling

How much can I earn?
You set your own prices. Devmarket takes 15% commission. Top sellers earn $5,000-$20,000 monthly.

When do I get paid?
Payouts process every Friday for the previous week's sales. Minimum payout is $50. Available methods: PayPal, bank transfer, or crypto.

Technical Support

For account issues: support@devmarket.io
For security concerns: security@devmarket.io
Response time: Under 24 hours for paid users, 48 hours for free accounts.
